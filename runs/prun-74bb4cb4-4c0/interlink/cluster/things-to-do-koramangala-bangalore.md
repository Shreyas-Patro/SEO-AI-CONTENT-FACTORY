## How Do I Spend a Day in Koramangala?

Start your Koramangala experience with the locality's thriving coffee culture. Specialty coffee and breakfast cafes operate throughout the area, with breakfast options available from early morning hours.

Peak morning foot traffic occurs 7–9 AM on weekdays, driven by office commuters and fitness center visits.
