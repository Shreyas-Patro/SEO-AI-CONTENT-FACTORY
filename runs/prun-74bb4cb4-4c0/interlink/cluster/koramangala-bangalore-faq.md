### History and Development FAQs

**What's the origin of the name 'Koramangala'?**

Koramangala's name originates from Kannada words 'kora' (shelter) and 'mangala' (prosperity). The area remained agricultural until the 1970s, when Bangalore's IT boom triggered rapid urbanization.

The transformation from village to startup hub happened within just four decades. Historical records show rapid development beginning in the 1980s with planned residential layouts.

**When did Koramangala develop into a commercial hub?**

Koramangala was developed as a residential locality in the 1980s-1990s, initially as a middle-class neighborhood before transitioning to a premium commercial hub [Source: Bangalore Development Authority (BDA) Historical Records, 2023]. The real commercial transformation began post-2010.

The locality saw significant commercial growth with over 800+ startups and SMEs establishing operations by 2024 [Source: Bangalore Chamber of Commerce & Industry (BCCI) Report, 2024]. Major infrastructure development including metro connectivity planning began in 2015, with Phase 2 extension towards Koramangala approved in 2019 [Source: Bangalore Metro Rail Corporation Limited (BMRCL), 2024].

**How has Koramangala's real estate developed over time?**

Real estate development accelerated from 2015 onwards, with cumulative commercial space additions of 2.8 million sq ft between 2015-2024 [Source: Knight Frank India Bangalore Market Report Q1 2024]. Residential property prices have appreciated 8-12% CAGR over 2019-2024.

Residential property prices have appreciated at 8-12% CAGR over 2019-2024, with 2-BHK apartments ranging from ₹85-₹150 lakhs [Source: RERA Karnataka Property Registry Data & PropTiger Analysis, 2024]. This consistent growth reflects strong demand and limited supply.
