# Brigade vs Prestige Bangalore: Which Builder is Better for Your Investment?

Choosing between Brigade and Prestige depends on your budget and what you value. Brigade offers better value pricing and mixed-use amenities (retail, offices, homes in one project); Prestige focuses on premium standalone residential in established localities like Koramangala and Indiranagar. Both are listed, financially stable developers, but they serve different buyer profiles [Source: Brigade Group in Bengaluru 2026 Res.txt].

If your budget is ₹80 lakhs to ₹1.5 crore and you want mixed-use amenities (retail, offices, homes) with 24–30 month possession, Brigade's value positioning works better. If you're investing ₹1.5 crore+ and want established resale demand in premium localities like Koramangala or Indiranagar with 30–36 month possession, Prestige's premium brand carries stronger buyer recognition and 8–12% faster resale [Source: Knight Frank Q1 2026].
