# Electronic City Metro Bangalore: Impact\n\nFor over a decade, Electronic City (E-City) has been defined by its job density and its peak hour traffic congestion. Although it supported the living of thousands of professionals, families and entrepreneurs, the real estate market became stagnant due to commute issues and old settlements.\n\nThe inauguration of Namma Metro Yellow Line ( RV Road- Bommasnadra) completely redefined the market transforming it from a stagnant zone to a self sustained, high growth investment zone.\n\nIn addition to the upcoming metro, the area has a well planned road network. The Elevated Expressway that connects Electronic City to Silk Board has drastically reduced the commute time, allowing professionals travel to and fro to work with ease. Local buses and cab services by companies like Uber, Ola help make the inter-city travel experience smoother.\n\nThis guide will deep dive into the current market reality and what makes Electronic City a new hotspot for both living and investment purposes.\n\n---\n## Key Features of BLR Electronic City\nE-city is spread across 330 acres and includes numerous IT and tech companies. Electronic city is divided into Phase I, Phase II, Phase III phases, each providing a well planned business environment. The presence of top of the line infrastructure, office spaces further strengthens its position . This area comprises Major residential societies by major developers like Prestige, Ajmera, Provident, Mahindra and many more. Each fulfilling the needs of the buyer from affordable housing to luxurious living. \n\n---\n## Connectivity of Electronic City\n\nThe main strength of Electronic City is Connectivity: \n\n* Hosur Road, along with the Flyover at Electronic City, saving time to the center of Bengaluru.  \n* NICE Ring Road that connects E-City to Mysore yard and Tumkur Road.  \n* Heelalige Railway Station is the nearest to Bangalore Electronic City, providing connection through suburban rail.  \n* Phase 2 of the Namma Metro, is also under development.\n\n* The distance from Bangalore Airport to Electronic City is around 56 km, which usually takes from 1.5 by road.\n\n---\n## Electronic City for the Home Buyers \u0026 Investors\n\nThe E-City is rooted in the combination of great infrastructure, security
