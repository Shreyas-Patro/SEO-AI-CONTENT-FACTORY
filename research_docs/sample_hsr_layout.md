# HSR Layout, Bangalore — Research Notes

## Overview
HSR Layout (short for Hosur-Sarjapur Road Layout) is a residential and commercial neighborhood in South-East Bangalore. It was developed by the Bangalore Development Authority (BDA) in the 1980s and has since become one of the most sought-after localities in the city.

## Demographics and Population
According to the 2021 BBMP ward data, HSR Layout falls under Ward 174 and Ward 175 with an estimated population of approximately 150,000 residents. The area has seen a 25% population growth between 2015 and 2023, driven primarily by the IT sector boom.

Source: BBMP Ward Statistics 2023, https://bbmp.gov.in/ward-data

## Real Estate Market
The average property price in HSR Layout ranges from ₹8,500 to ₹12,000 per square foot as of Q4 2025, according to data from the Karnataka Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA).

The rental market is equally strong:
- 1BHK apartments: ₹12,000 - ₹18,000 per month
- 2BHK apartments: ₹22,000 - ₹35,000 per month
- 3BHK apartments: ₹35,000 - ₹55,000 per month

Source: Karnataka RERA Database 2025

## Infrastructure
HSR Layout is served by the Bommanahalli Metro Station on the Yellow Line (opened 2024). The Outer Ring Road provides connectivity to major IT hubs including Electronic City, Whitefield, and Manyata Tech Park.

The area has 12 public parks maintained by BBMP, including the popular HSR BDA Complex Park spanning 4.5 acres.

Source: BMRCL Metro Map 2024, BBMP Parks Directory 2025
