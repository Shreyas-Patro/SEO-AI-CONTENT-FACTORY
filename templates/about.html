About

We're building topical authority, not blog posts.

Canvas Homes is a one-person operation competing with MagicBricks and NoBroker for the most valuable real-estate searches in Bangalore. The advantage isn't headcount — it's a pipeline that produces clusters of deeply interlinked, fact-checked, schema-tagged articles faster than any traditional content team.

Every article is written by the agent swarm, verified against a private research corpus, and approved by a human at three different gates. The result: content that ranks on Google and shows up as the cited answer when someone asks Perplexity or ChatGPT about Sarjapur Road.

Strategy

The three pillars.

01

Trust through provenance

Backlinks plus dense interlinking, credible author personas with real LinkedIn presence, and every statistic cited to a primary source. The Fact Verifier agent rejects any claim it can't trace.

02

Q&A coverage at scale

Hub → spoke → sub-spoke architecture, paired with FAQ schema on every page. We become the default answer for AI engines (Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude, Google AIO) and for voice search.

03

Compounding internal authority

The Link Engine threads semantic + lexical links through every new cluster, so each new article makes every existing one rank a little higher. PageRank goes up monotonically.
