01 / What it does

A pipeline, not a chatbot.

L1 · Discovery

Trend Scout, Competitor Spy, Keyword Mapper

Finds what people actually search for, what your competitors rank for, and which keywords have a path to page one.

L2 · Architecture

Content Architect, FAQ Architect, Research Prompts

Designs the cluster: a hub article, spoke articles, sub-spokes, and the FAQ matrix that feeds the AEO answer engines.

L3 · Writing

Lead Writer, Fact Verifier, Brand Auditor, Rewriter

Drafts each article, checks every claim against your research corpus, audits brand tone, and rewrites until both pass the gate.

L4 · Distribution

Meta Tagger, Interlinker, Schema

Generates schema.org markup, picks the canonical URL, and threads deep internal links through the cluster — semantic and lexical.

02 / Why it wins

Trust, not tricks.

Topical authority

Hub → spoke → sub-spoke

Every cluster is a tree, not a list. Internal links flow from generalist hubs down to specialist sub-spokes the way the Knowledge Graph expects.

Cited statistics

Primary sources only

Numbers are pulled from your research corpus and tagged with provenance. The Fact Verifier rejects claims that can't be backed.

AEO-ready

FAQ schema, structured answers

Every article ships with FAQPage schema, breadcrumbs, and Article structured data — the formats AI engines actually parse.
